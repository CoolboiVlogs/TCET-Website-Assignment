TCET (Thakur College of Engineering and Technology) has received several prestigious awards and recognitions in the field of education. These include the IMC - Ramkrishna Bajaj National Quality Commendation Certification in 2012 and the IMC - Ramkrishna Bajaj National Quality Award in 2015. In 2016, TCET was selected for the Asia Pacific Quality Award (APQA) for the best in class in the education category. The college has also been honored as an excellent performing and innovative engineering institute in Maharashtra at the National Maharashtra Education Summit & Awards 2016. TCET is actively engaged in industry-institute interaction and has memberships in the National Entrepreneurship Network (NEN) and Confederation of Indian Industry (CII). The college is known for its application of modern technology, effective governance, and various initiatives such as the teacher-guardian scheme and book bank scheme. TCET has established itself as one of the leading engineering colleges affiliated with the University of Mumbai in Maharashtra in just 20 years of its existence.
